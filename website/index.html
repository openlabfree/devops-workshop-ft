About FemTech Nepal

Our Mission

We believe that technology has the power to transform lives and create opportunities. Our mission is to empower women in Nepal by providing them with the skills, knowledge, and support they need to thrive in the digital age.

Through our comprehensive programs, we address the unique challenges faced by women in tech, from education and training to career development and networking opportunities.

We're building a community where women can learn, grow, and succeed together in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Our Services

Tech Education

Comprehensive coding bootcamps, workshops, and courses designed specifically for women, covering programming, web development, and emerging technologies.

Mentorship Program

One-on-one mentoring from experienced women in tech, providing guidance, career advice, and professional development support.

Career Development

Job placement assistance, resume building, interview preparation, and networking opportunities with leading tech companies in Nepal.

Community Support

Regular meetups, networking events, and a supportive community where women can connect, share experiences, and grow together.

Startup Incubation

Support for women entrepreneurs with innovative tech ideas, including funding guidance, business development, and technical mentorship.

Digital Literacy

Basic computer skills, internet safety, and digital tools training to help women navigate the digital world confidently.
